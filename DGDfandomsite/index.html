Dance Gavin Dance

Dance Gavin Dance is a band from Sacramento, California, formed in 2005. Noted for creating a genre of music called "Swancore" named after Guitarist Will Swan.

Although they have been a band since 2005 they havent always been the same, they have had quite a bit of vocalist changes throught the years.
