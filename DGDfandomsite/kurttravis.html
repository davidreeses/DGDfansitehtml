After Jonny Craig left the band Kurt Travis was his replacment. August 19, 2008 the band released first album with Kurt. Self-titled but called "Death Star" due to cover art. Supported by four singles; The Robot with Human Hair Pt. 3, Alex English, Me and Zoloft Get Along Just Fine and Caviar featuring Chino Moreno from Deftones.

June 9, 2009. The band released the last album with Kurt named Happiness supported by three singles; Don't Tell Dave, NASA and Tree Village.

In 2010 Kurt left the band and Jonny Craig returned.
