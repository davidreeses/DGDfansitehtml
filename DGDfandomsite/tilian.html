October 8, 2013 the band released Acceptance Speech. The first studio album featuring Tilian. Supported by five singles; The Robot with Human Hair, Pt. 4, Acceptance Speech, Jesus H. Macy, Strawberry Swisher Pt. 3 and Death of the Robot with Human Hair.

April 14, 2015 the band released Instant Gratification supported by 4 singles; On The Run, We Own The Night, Stroke God, Millionare and Eagle vs. Crows.

October 7, 2016 the band released Mothership supported by four singles; Chucky vs. The Giant Tortoise, Betrayed by the Game, Young Robot, Inspire the Liars.

June 8, 2018 the band released Artificial Selection supported by five singles; Midnight Crusade, Son of Robot, Care, Count Bassy and Hair Song.

April 24, 2020 the band released Afterburner supported by five singles; Prisoner, Strawberry's Wake, Lyrics Lie, Three Wishes and One In A Million,

July 29, 2022 the band released Jackpot Juicer supported by five singles; Synergy, Pop Off!, Die Another Day, Cream Of the Crop and Feels Bad Man.
